San Francisco has a high proportion of homeless
San Francisco is one of the cities with the highest proportion of homeless to the general population, and many of those homeless are unsheltered. The size of the circles is proportional to the number of unsheltered homeless per 100,000 county residents. Note that we did not display data for New York City or Los Angeles.
