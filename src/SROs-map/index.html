Code violations at housing for the homeless
The Crosby Hotel had by far the most building code violations among supportive housing sites in San Francisco from the start of 2015 to August 2016. The large number of violations reflect ongoing maintenance issues, but also tenants’ concerted efforts to report problems. The number of violations at the Crosby has decreased in 2016, suggesting that efforts to maintain the hotel are improving. Compare the Crosby to other supportive housing and all other cited properties in the city in that period by hovering over the dots on the map below.
