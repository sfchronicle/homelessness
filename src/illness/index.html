The health conditions plaguing SF’s homeless population
Many of the homeless individuals in San Francisco grapple with a confluence of mental health, medical and substance abuse issues. The most medically challenged subset of the population – the 338 people accessing the most emergency services – are far more likely to have a complex combination of health ailments.
