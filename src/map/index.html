Complaints about the homeless are rising in SF
Residents in SF can register complaints about the homeless through 311, and such reports have been proliferating at an extremely high rate. Police, on the other hand, have been issuing standard numbers of citations.

Homeless complaints are a growing proportion of 311 complaints
San Francisco residents have been submitting increasing numbers of 311 complaints in the last 4 years. Data for 2016 is not complete, yet it already eclipses previous years. Homeless complaints have been a growing proportion of these complaints, reflecting the public's increasing frustration with the homeless population.
Police dispatches are increasing
Police are being dispatched more and more for homeless-related and sit-lie complaints. A small proportion of these responses result in citations or arrests, and these numbers have remained relatively constant over the last three years.
